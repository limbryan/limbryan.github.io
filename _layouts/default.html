I am a PhD student at the Adaptive and Intelligent Robotics Lab, part of the Department of Computing at Imperial College London, where I work on robotics and machine learning. My PhD advisor is Antoine Cully.

Previously, I completed my MEng year at MIT, where I worked with Prof. Alberto Rodriguez and Prof. Sangbae Kim. I have an MEng in Mechanical Engineering from Imperial College London.
